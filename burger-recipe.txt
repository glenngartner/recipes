This is my burger recipe: 

1 lb ground beef
1 tsp cumin
1/2 all spice
1 tsp salt
.4 tsp pepper
1 tsp paprika

smack the patties into hocky pucks. The thinner the better. 
using a cast-iron skillet, heat some ghee in a pan over medium-high heat. 
fry the burger for 2 minutes on each side. 

enjoy!
